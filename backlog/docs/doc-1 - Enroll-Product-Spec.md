# Enroll — Product Spec

## Problem

Big brands like Starbucks, Sephora, and McDonald's invest hundreds of millions of dollars perfecting their loyalty technology and programs. Small service businesses — salons, spas, barbershops, clinics — don't have access to that level of expertise, tooling, or budget.

As a result, loyalty for SMBs is often:

- Generic and one-size-fits-all
- Manually managed or poorly integrated
- Focused on simple punch cards or basic discounts
- Ineffective at driving true retention and recurring revenue

## Solution

Enroll is an AI-first B2B SaaS built specifically for service businesses. Instead of offering rigid templates, Enroll automatically analyzes each business and generates a tailored loyalty program aligned with its brand, services, and goals.

The vision: give small businesses access to enterprise-grade loyalty — without the enterprise price tag.

## POC Hypothesis

> AI can automatically understand a service business and generate a meaningful loyalty foundation in minutes.

This POC validates the core hypothesis by demonstrating end-to-end: from business onboarding → AI analysis → loyalty program generation → program preview.

---

## Target Users

**Primary:** Franchise operators or multi-location service chains looking to standardize loyalty across locations without enterprise tooling costs.

**Secondary:** Owners and operators of small service businesses

- Salons, spas, barbershops
- Medical/aesthetic clinics
- Fitness studios, wellness centers
- Independent service providers

## Core Concepts

### Business Profile

A structured representation of a service business: name, category, services offered, average ticket size, brand tone, and customer demographics. Used as the context for AI-generated loyalty programs.

### Loyalty Program

A tailored set of rules, rewards, and mechanics generated by AI based on the business profile. Includes:

- **Earn mechanics** — how customers accumulate points or credits (e.g., per visit, per dollar spent, per service type)
- **Reward tiers** — thresholds and corresponding rewards (e.g., free service, discount, exclusive access)
- **Referral** — referral mechanics and rewards
- **Bonus rules** — multipliers or special conditions (e.g., birthday bonuses, referral rewards, seasonal campaigns)
- **Brand voice** — naming conventions and messaging tone aligned with the business identity

### Program Preview

A read-only view of the generated program that the business owner can review before activating. Designed to feel polished enough to validate desirability.

## POC Scope

### In Scope

- Business onboarding flow (name, category, services, goals)
- AI-powered loyalty program generation from business profile
- Program preview UI (tiers, earn rules, rewards)
- Basic ability to regenerate or tweak the program
- Customer-facing loyalty enrollment and tracking

### Out of Scope (Post-POC)

- POS or booking system integrations
- Payment or billing
- Multi-location management
- Analytics and reporting dashboard
- Mobile app

---

## Key User Flow (POC)

1. **Onboard** — Business owner enters basic info (name, category, services, goals)
2. **Analyze** — AI processes the business profile and identifies relevant loyalty mechanics
3. **Generate** — A complete loyalty program draft is created and displayed
4. **Review** — Owner browses the generated tiers, rewards, and earn rules
5. **Refine** — Owner can provide feedback or regenerate with adjusted inputs

## Design Principles

- **Fast to value** — A business owner should see their program within minutes, not hours
- **Feels tailored** — The output should feel like it was made for their specific business, not a template
- **Trustworthy** — Language and structure should feel professional and credible
- **Low friction** — No technical knowledge required from the operator
