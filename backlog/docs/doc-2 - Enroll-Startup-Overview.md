# Enroll — startup overview

## One-line pitch

Loyalty programs for SMBs — acquire, retain, and grow customers with one streamlined platform.

## 60-second pitch

Big brands like Starbucks, Sephora, and McDonald's spend hundreds of millions perfecting their loyalty programs tech and program. With Enroll, small businesses get that same level of expertise — without the price tag.

Enroll is an AI-first B2B SaaS designed to help service businesses grow through smarter loyalty and subscriptions. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. Every program is tailored to fit your brand's DNA, driving real retention and recurring revenue.

Backed by over a decade of experience as a product designer, educator, and founder of a successful art business, Enroll was built to empower business owners with the tools usually reserved for industry giants.

Whether you're a service business looking to scale or a customer looking for value — Enroll makes subscriptions simple, profitable, and personal.

## The problem

It's still a pain for SMBs to:

- **Retain customers** — lasting relationships lead to loyal, profitable customers and organic growth, but most SMBs have no systematic way to build them
- **Reduce operational cost** — labor is ~70% of spending, yet no automated loyalty solution exists for service-based SMBs, leading to inefficient operations

Customer loyalty specifically is difficult because:

- Only 32% of consumers find loyalty offers relevant today
- 69% of consumers are looking for personalized offers
- Top-tier loyalty programs cost ~$200K/year — far beyond SMB budgets
- Off-the-shelf: $6K/year (basic, rigid); white-label: $50K/year; custom-built: $200K/year
- Key cost drivers: development (<$200K/year), customer support (~$50K/year), data analysis (~$100K/year)

Beauty industry data specifically:

- Only 45% of clients return after their first appointment
- Best-in-class salons convert only 57% of first-time visits into a third appointment
- 30% revenue variance between peak and off-peak seasons
- Average staffing costs: $195K

Most existing loyalty programs start at POS with a tempting discount but fizzle out — no ongoing engagement, no personalization, no retention loop.

## The solution

Enroll is an **AI-powered loyalty-first CRM** for service businesses. It:

- Creates and manages tailored loyalty programs (type, tiers, rewards)
- Automates customer engagement and rewards
- Manages payments and subscriptions
- Gets businesses fully operational within 2–3 minutes via AI-powered onboarding

### How it works

**Onboarding** — Chat-based setup powered by AI. The business owner describes their business or uploads a menu/service PDF. AI parses it, extracts services and pricing, and generates a loyalty program within minutes.

**Loyalty program** — Tailored and evolving. Combines:
- Points: spend more, get more (e.g. 1 point per $1 spent)
- Tiers: Silver (5% off), Gold (10% off + birthday gift)
- Bonus rules: signup discounts, double points on weekdays, referral bonuses
- Refreshing rewards: timely surprises to maintain engagement

**Customer engagement** — AI identifies crucial customer journey points (activation, churn risk) and triggers the right reward at the right time. Anti-churn suggestions, reactivation bonuses, and automated campaigns (welcome, birthday, win-back) run without manual effort.

**AI assistant** — Real-time alerts and performance insights, behavior-based marketing targeting, AI-generated copy for social and email, and predictive churn intervention.

**Vendor dashboard** — Business owners track key metrics, manage services, bookings, and loyalty programs from one place.

## Competitive positioning

Existing solutions fall into two camps:

- **Expensive and smart** — Salesforce Loyalty Cloud, Oracle CrowdTwist, Zinrelo Enterprise: powerful but built for enterprises, priced accordingly
- **Affordable but basic** — Smile.io, TapMango, Marsello, Fresha, Mindbody: off-the-shelf tools that lack AI personalization and are focused on eCommerce, not service businesses

**Enroll's position: affordable and smart.** Competitors haven't yet applied GenAI to eliminate the complexity that makes flexible loyalty hard.

No existing solution provides a plug-and-play loyalty platform purpose-built for service SMBs with marketing, scheduling, billing, and AI personalization in one place.

## Product vision

**Two deployment modes:**

- **Standalone** — full-cycle solution covering services, payments, and loyalty
- **Integrated** — plugs into existing CRM, POS, and booking tools, adding loyalty, subscriptions, and engagement on top

**Minimum loveable product:** Enroll needs to be the easiest way for a customer to find, subscribe, and repeatedly book a service.

**Long-term:** Develop SMB loyalty programs that benefit both sides — businesses grow with recurring income and stronger customer bonds; customers subscribe to services they love and get rewarded.
